The Best Movies According to Kingsley

My top 3 movies of all time

Avatar the last airbender

A beautiful animation with a simple colorful world and compelling stories.
This is my best animated movie or series.

Godzilla Vs Kong

I always loved Kaiju movies, and this so far has been the best out of the bunch.
It might be second best soon though, judging by the trailers of "Godzilla X Kong: The new empire".

Avengers Infinity War and Endgame

Yeah, i like superhero movies, but it has to have meaningful stories, a good CGI,
and it should be able to make the audience emotional, and Avengers Infinity War and Endgame ticks all these criterias.
